Web Inspiration

Site Inspire is a huge website filled with different projects to inspire you. They base their showcased websites on cleanliness and simplicity. You are able to become a member to receive weekly emails and be able to submit your own site to be featured.

Admire the Web is filled with distinctive showcases for you to look at and be inspired by. Through their very active social media posts, they keep everyone up to date on the latest clean, modern, and simplistic designs. They change their showcase daily, so there is always fresh content to look over.

This is a unique site compared to others I have explored. They have a grading system for each site their feature based on design, usability, creativity, content, and developer. “A meeting point, where digital design professionals from across the globe find inspiration, impart knowledge and experience, connect, and share constructive, respectful critiques.

When you visit Behance, you are welcomed by a massive list of websites and designs they are featuring. They want to showcase and discover creative work for people to find inspiration through. You can login to your adobe account and manage your work through Behance.

This is volume three of a very successful line of books filled with themes, trends, and styles for web designers to look over. Amazon states that this book, “includes more than 700 websites arranged thematically, so you can find inspiration for layout, color, style and more.” The sites in the book are all organized by type, design style, theme, color, element, and structure.
